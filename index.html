Frequently Asked Questions

How does MyLifeEasy PRIME work?

+

MyLifeEasy PRIME is an annual subscription that gives you exclusive discounts, priority access, and premium features across all our services. Pay once and save throughout the year on accommodation, food, repairs, courses, and more. Most users recover their subscription cost within the first month!

Can I try before I buy?

+

Yes! Start with our free Basic plan to explore the platform. You can upgrade to PRIME anytime to unlock discounts and premium features. We also offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on PRIME subscriptions.

Are all service providers verified?

+

Absolutely! Every PG owner, service provider, and business on our platform goes through a strict verification process. We check credentials, visit properties, verify licenses, and collect genuine reviews to ensure you get safe, quality services.

How do payments work?

+

We use secure payment gateways for all transactions. You can pay via UPI, cards, net banking, or wallets. For services, you can choose to pay directly to providers or through our platform with added buyer protection.

What if I'm not satisfied with a service?

+

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you're not happy with any service, contact our support team within 24 hours. We'll work with the provider to resolve the issue or provide alternatives. PRIME members get priority resolution.

About MyLifeEasy

Building India's first complete life ecosystem

Our Story

Every year, millions of students and working professionals move to new cities, chasing dreams of education and career growth. But the excitement of new opportunities quickly gives way to the stress of finding accommodation, managing daily needs, and navigating an unfamiliar city.

We saw friends struggle with unreliable PG owners, expensive food delivery, difficult access to basic services, and the overwhelming challenge of managing it all. That's when we realized - people don't need more apps, they need one complete ecosystem that truly understands and solves their daily challenges.

MyLifeEasy was born from this insight. We're not just another booking platform or service marketplace. We're building a comprehensive life management ecosystem that brings together everything you need to thrive in a new city - from finding the perfect accommodation to advancing your career, all powered by intelligent AI that learns and adapts to your needs.

The Problem We're Solving

Today's students and professionals face fragmented solutions. They juggle multiple apps for different needs, waste time comparing options across platforms, pay high fees to middlemen and brokers, struggle to verify service quality and safety, and feel isolated without proper community support.

This fragmentation doesn't just waste time and money - it creates stress, reduces productivity, and prevents people from fully enjoying their new city experience. We believe life should be easier than this.

Our Vision

To build India's first complete life ecosystem that empowers students and professionals by simplifying daily living, promoting growth, and creating a trustworthy digital environment where everyone can thrive.

Our Mission

To make life easier through technology, AI, and seamless access to essential services - anytime, anywhere. We're committed to reducing stress, lowering costs, and building a supportive community for everyone navigating life in a new city.

What Makes Us Different

Complete Integration: Unlike platforms that focus on just one aspect of life, MyLifeEasy brings together accommodation, daily services, education, career growth, and lifestyle in one unified experience. No more app-hopping or account juggling.

AI-Powered Intelligence: Our platform learns from your preferences and behavior to provide increasingly personalized recommendations. From finding the perfect PG to suggesting the right courses for your career goals, our AI works for you 24/7.

Verified & Trusted Network: Every service provider on our platform is thoroughly verified. We personally visit properties, check credentials, verify licenses, and maintain ongoing quality monitoring to ensure your safety and satisfaction.

Community-First Approach: We're not just connecting you to services - we're building a supportive community. Meet fellow students and professionals, discover city events, share experiences, and build meaningful connections.

Genuine Savings: With zero brokerage fees, exclusive discounts, smart bundling, and our PRIME membership benefits, users save an average of ‚Çπ24,000 to ‚Çπ84,000 annually. That's money back in your pocket for what truly matters.

Our Commitment

We're committed to making life genuinely easier for millions of students and professionals across India. This means continuously improving our platform based on your feedback, expanding our verified service network to more cities, maintaining the highest standards of safety and quality, keeping our pricing transparent and fair, and investing in technology that truly serves your needs.

We measure our success not in downloads or transactions, but in the stress we eliminate, the money we help you save, and the opportunities we help you discover.
